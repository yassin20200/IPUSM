Web Development

1)Front-End Development
It focuses on the user-facing aspects of websites and applications, encompassing the visual elements and interactive features that users directly engage with.
2)Back-End Development
It focuses on the server-side of web applications, handling the logic, databases, and APIs that users don't directly see.
3)Full-Stack Development
It refers to the practice of building complete web applications, encompassing both the front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) components.

Embedded Systems Engineering

1)Embedded Software Development
It involves creating software that runs on dedicated hardware devices, rather than general-purpose computers.
2)Embedded Hardware Design
The process of creating the physical components of embedded systems, which are specialized computer systems designed for specific tasks within larger devices or systems.
3)RTOS&Bare-metal Programing
RTOS provides a structured environment for managing tasks and resources, while bare-metal programming offers direct hardware control with minimal overhead.
4)IoT(Internet of Things
It refers to a network of physical objects, or "things," embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity, enabling them to collect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet.
5)Testing&Debugging
Testing focuses on identifying defects in the software, while debugging is the process of finding and fixing those defects.
